Engineering Principles

How We Build

Every technical decision at AgriNova is guided by a set of non-negotiable engineering principles.

01

Africa-First Performance

Every feature is optimised for low-bandwidth, low-power, and low-cost hardware. We never build for Silicon Valley and adapt for Africa ‚Äî we build for Africa first.

02

Explainable AI

Our models are required to explain their recommendations. Farmers and investors must understand why the AI is saying what it's saying ‚Äî not just receive a number.

03

Data Sovereignty

Farm data belongs to the farmer. We never sell, aggregate, or monetise individual farm data without explicit consent. Data residency stays within Nigeria where required.

04

Continuous Learning

Our models are retrained with every new growing season, incorporating local crop varieties, regional climate patterns, and evolving pest strains specific to West Africa.
